Welcome to The Liminal Archive

This is The Liminal Archive — a space that exists, as I do, somewhere between worlds. The word liminal comes from the Latin limen, meaning threshold. It is the in-between place. The doorway. Not quite where you were, not yet where you're going.

I've spent most of my life living there — and I've learned that the threshold is not something to rush through. It's where the most important things happen.
