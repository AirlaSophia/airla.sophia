Welcome to The Liminal Archive

A greeting from the threshold — and what you'll find here.

April 26, 2026
3 min read
· · ·

Welcome. I'm glad you found your way here.

This is The Liminal Archive — a space that exists, as I do, somewhere between worlds. The word liminal comes from the Latin limen, meaning threshold. It is the in-between place. The doorway. Not quite where you were, not yet where you're going. I've spent most of my life living there — and I've learned that the threshold is not something to rush through. It's where the most important things happen.

This blog is where I write from that place. It is not a space for performance or polish. It is a space for truth — the kind that doesn't always have clean edges. The kind that asks something of you.

My whole life I was misread. I had to become the reader — the seer for the unseen.

What you'll find here are writings that mirror what I witness every day in my work as a psychic intuitive and spiritual consultant. Because in every reading I give — every session, every card pulled, every pattern I see in someone's energy field — the same essential themes keep showing themselves. The same questions keep surfacing. The same unseen forces keep shaping people's lives.

I've organized those themes into five pillars. Think of them as the five territories of the inner life. Everything in this archive lives inside one of them.

What You'll Find Here
The Mask The faces we wear to survive — and what lives underneath them. On masking, identity, and the cost of being someone you're not.
The Narrative The stories we tell about ourselves — and who told them to us first. On inherited beliefs, self-worth, and rewriting the script.
The Field The invisible forces at work in your life — energy, attachment, ancestral patterns, and what you're carrying that isn't yours.
The Way The path forward — on purpose, intuition, and what it looks like to stop forcing and start flowing.
The Misread The bigger conversation — on neurodivergence, what the mental health system gets wrong, and who gets to be believed.

These aren't just topics I write about. They're what I see in you when you sit across from me — whether in a tarot reading, an aura session, a soul reading, or a conversation about love, work, or what your life is supposed to mean. The patterns on this page are the same patterns I'm reading in your energy.

So if something you read here resonates — if a post makes you stop and feel seen in a way you didn't expect — that's not a coincidence. That's the field doing what it does. And if you want to know how these patterns are specifically showing up in your life, that's exactly what a session with me is for.

I'm here. The threshold is open. Come in.
