About Airla

Where the Mystical Meets
the Deeply Human

Most people arrive here looking for an answer. A relationship that won’t resolve itself. A path that won’t come clear. A feeling they can’t name but can’t ignore. Something in them already knows — they just need someone who can see.

That is what I do.

I’m Melissa, known in this work as Airla Sophia — psychic intuitive, seer, energy healer, and author. My readings draw on psychic intuition, tarot, charm casting, geomancy, aura and energy field reading, chakra work, cord and attachment reading, ancestral lineage, Akashic soul reading, dream and symbol interpretation, and remote viewing. Each session is shaped entirely by what you need.

I am also a late-diagnosed autistic and ADHD woman — and that is not incidental to this work. My whole life I was misread. By systems, by practitioners, by people who couldn’t see what was actually there. That experience became my greatest gift: I learned to read what others miss. To see the unseen. To name what has no name yet.

My books — Misread, The Wu Wei Workbook, The Human Instrument, and The Ripple Effect — extend this work into the page. They are for those who have always sensed they were built differently, and are ready to understand why that is not a flaw.

You don’t have to explain yourself here. You just have to show up.
